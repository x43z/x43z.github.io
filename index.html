Cloudvale

In this unique pixel art adventure, you will explore a forgotten land in search of valuable resources and new recruits to help you survive.

As you journey through the land, you will encounter challenging combat and intense bossfights that will test your mettle. But fear not, as you progress through the game, you will be able to upgrade your character and weapons, giving you the edge you need to overcome even the toughest of foes.

In addition to fighting, you will also have the opportunity to engage in farming, providing you with the resources you need to survive. And as you settle into an abandoned town, you will have the chance to recruit NPCs who can provide you with valuable goods and services, helping you build a thriving community.

So grab your weapon and embark on an epic adventure in "Cloudvale"!

Pushing Crates

Pushing Crates is a sliding puzzle game where your goal is to move the blue crate to the marked goal.

Play 100 levels of varying difficulty in any order.

Try to solve each level in the minimal amount of moves for an additional challenge.
